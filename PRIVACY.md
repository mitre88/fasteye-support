# FastEye Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 3, 2026

FastEye is designed to provide a local, on-device cognitive training experience.

## Data Collection
FastEye does not require account registration and does not collect personal data for the core app experience.

## On-Device Processing
On compatible Apple devices, some word generation features may use Apple on-device Foundation Models. Prompts and results used for those features are processed locally on the device.

## Analytics and Tracking
FastEye does not use third-party tracking SDKs and does not track users across apps or websites.

## Permissions
FastEye does not require camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photo library access for the main app experience.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact: support@fast-eye.app
